That's it (for now) - Farewell from bitswap

Dear valued customers,

It's with a heavy heart that we announce the closure of bitswap. This project has been an incredible journey for us, and we are immensely grateful for the opportunity to serve you.

Unfortunately, despite our best efforts, we've reached a point where continuing is no longer feasible. While it's a difficult decision, we believe it's the right one given the circumstances.

We want to express our sincerest apologies for any inconvenience this may cause. We truly wish things could have turned out differently.

If you have any questions, feedback, or simply want to reminisce about our time together, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at yannpiot+bitswap@gmail.com. We'd love to hear from you one last time.

Thank you for your support, understanding, and for being part of the bitswap community. Though this chapter may be closing, we hope our paths cross again in the future.

Warm regards,

The bitswap Team
